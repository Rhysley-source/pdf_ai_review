Overview
This is a Service Agreement between Acme Corp ("Client") and Vendor Ltd ("Provider") for the provision of software development services, effective January 1, 2025. The document spans 42 pages and is governed by English law, with disputes to be resolved in the courts of London. The agreement establishes the commercial terms, obligations, and risk allocation between both parties for the duration of the engagement.
Summary
The contract sets out standard commercial terms for a software development engagement. Payment is due within 30 days of invoice, with late interest at 1.5% per month. Provider's liability is heavily capped at three months of fees with all indirect damages excluded — a significant concern for Client. The indemnification clause is one-sided, placing most exposure on Client. IP ownership transfers to Client only upon full payment. Confidentiality obligations run for five years post-termination, and either party may terminate with 30 days' written notice.
